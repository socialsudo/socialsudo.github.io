MY FIRST ANIMATION

"Marketing of Orbit Shifters and Shift Enablers via Animation"

  • Explore Project

PROJECT GOALS

Cognizant academy have batches containing exceptionally hard working and talented students. They are digilant and diciplined in thier work. Such batches are called Orbit Shifters and Shift Enablers. We felt that their hard work prior to joining must not go unnoticed or unrewarded. So, this project, proposed by our manager, aimed at creating an animation video to market the idea of such batches and to highlight such talent.

RESPONSIBILITIES

The first step to making this animation video possible was to write down a script that would have a storyline highlighting all the key factors of a trainees' journey pre and post Joining, and also the main features of "Early Engagement". Early Engagement is a learning portal available to joinees pre-joining.
According to the script A total of twenty three panels of scenes were traced to be animated.The heart of any story is its characters and the color schemes used in the story that would keep the viewer immersed in the video. All the twenty-three scenes were properly traced in layers, ready to be animated.
The traced scenes were properly Animated according to the storyline requirements. The animation was then ready to tell the world its story.

TECHNOLOGIES USED

Adobe Flash
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe Photoshop

LEARNINGS

I learnt a lot from this project, specially the basics of Animation like Squash and stretch, Anticipation, Staging and much more about animation.
This was my very first project in Animation and I am very thankful to Cognizant and specially Mr Suresh Kumar, who recognised my talent and passion about movie making and animation and gave me the oportunity to work on this project.
