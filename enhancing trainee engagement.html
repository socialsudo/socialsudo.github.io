ENHANCING TRAINEE ENGAGEMENT

"How might we improve the engagement experience of graduate trainees
within the first 365 days (-90 to + 275) using digital solutions."

  • Explore Project

PROJECT GOALS

The main goal of this project was to learn about the trainee's needs and find digital solution to fulfill those needs. This was a fun project to build a better understanding of the design thinking methodology, but also a great way to solve the problems faced by trainees pre and post joining. The team enjoyed the effort and I was able to learn more about User research and prototyping the ideas from the Design Thinking Workshop.

JOURNEY MAP

From the collected data we created a timeline for the user. From -90 to +275 days, which included pre-joining formalities, Joining day Induction, Assessments, Learning schedule, Graduation, Buisness unit Transition and Dovetail assessment. We also created a bard where we categorized the things and ideas in three columns I Like, I Wish and What If. This gave us knowledge of what there is in Academy that users like and can be improved with help of "I Wish" column and "What If" column contained the great ideas.

IDEAS TO REALITY

Now this was the time to bring ideas into reality where my team came up with a prototype. Using this aplication trainee can enjoy the training phase as we made it very interactive by adding features like Select Technology, Techno Meter and Game on. One of the features of this prototype that was appriciated the most was Game On. Rather than the monotonous concept of assessments, we tried to tackle this area with a gamified approach.
"WHAT WE LEARN WITH PLEASURE WE NEVER FORGET"

LEARNINGS

Design Thinking workshop was just of 2 days, but I learnt alot in these 2 days. Through Design thinking workshop i was enlightened on how to approach a problem and dissove any preconceived notions about the problem itself. It taught me how to keep onself objective while tackling a problem. As now I know how to view a problem and how to generate a solution for that problem by looking at all the aspects of the problem.I learnt that how a simple idea can also help in building a great solution.
